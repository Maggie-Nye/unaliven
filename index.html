Hi, my name is Kevin, obviously. I live in Chicago now, but I didn’t always. Also obviously. I grew in a place the internet's never heard of, and that's Waterloo, Indiana. I thought it was Waterloo that was boring, but now that I'm in Chicago and it feels pretty much the same but more expensive, I think I might be the boring one.

Who am I? I’m a smoker. I smoke about a pack every 3 days. That’s not how people usually introduce themselves, I know, but it’s kind of the main thing about this blog, and maybe about me, too.

I’ve been smoking since I was a kid. It kind of runs in the family (Hi mom!). I’ve been smoking Marlboro Golds for a long time now, and I’m ready to break out of my rut. There are enough food blogs in the world, but I’ve never seen a cigarette blog, so enter: Cigs in the City, a blog for shitheads who want to watch me smoke myself to death. Kidding! I don’t really know who it’s a blog for yet, other than me. But if you're reading this and you have a request, leave me a comment. Maybe I'll read it.
